### What does this mod add?  
**This mod adds many challenges I've made and packs them together in a single mod**  
**Air Challenge** -> kills you whenever the block at your coordinates is air  
**Hunger Challenge** -> prevents you from increasing your hunger bar level or saturation  
**Light Challenge** -> damages you the equivalent of the light level at your block, this challenge has a "pure" version which is to remove the damage decrease that has been added to allow the player to not die when not underground  
**Xp Challenge** -> gaining xp kills you  
...

### How can it be configured?
Simply open the Mod Menu and look for the mod, open the config and enable or disable the challenges
